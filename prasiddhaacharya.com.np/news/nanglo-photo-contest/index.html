Swasika & Prabeshika became winner

May 1, 2023

  Prasiddha Acharya

On the occasion of New Year, a photo contest was organized on Facebook's "Nanglo" page from Chaitra 30 to Baisakh 15. To participate in this contest, contestants had to send their photos and the photo & the photo with the most likes would be declared the winner and accordingly the contest was conducted from Chaitra 30 to Baisakh 15.


In order to be a winner in that competition, you had to get complete real likes, no auto likes and fake likes would be accepted, if any contestant was found to have used auto likes in their photos, there was a rule that the contestants would be automatically expelled from the competition and accordingly, they would use auto likes in their photos. Contestants were even kicked out of the competition!

"Contestant No. 10, Swastika Ghimire" has won the contest with the most likes, while "Contestant No. 35, Prabeshika Acharya" has become the runner-up.


The winners will receive the following prizes:-
1. "HKR video & photography"
2. 2000 cash to the winner and 1000 cash to the runner-up sponsored by "NS Entertainment"
3. 1000 cash to the winner and 500 cash to the runner-up sponsored by "Nanglo"

The winner will receive a total cash of Rs. 5000, photo frame along with various gifts and runners-up will get a cash prize of Rs. 2500, you will get various gifts along with photo frames. Congratulations and good luck to the winners and runners-up ðŸŽ‰
