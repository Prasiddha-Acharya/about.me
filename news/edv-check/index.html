What to do if you forgot your Confirmation Number ?

Confirmation Number is 16 digit number which you had obtained after you submited your EDV application successfully. If you have lost this number then there is separate way to find it out :


- First you need to open the official website of EDV Lottery https://dvprogram.state.gov
- Click on Check Status Button.
- After you click on Continue button, you will see "Forgot Confirmation Number"
- Click on it & follow the steps:


1. Select the DV program that you had applied for

2. Fill up your Last/Family Name, First Name and Middle Name as shown above.

3. Fill up your Date Of Birth in A.D Month Day and Year respectively.

4. Provide your same email address that you provided while filling up your EDV application form before.

5. Click on Submit button it will automatically show your Confirmation number.

Important Facts for DV Lottery Registration



The US Embassy does not email the selected candidates nor will it publicize a list of selectees. You need to check EDV result by yourself.

The emails arrive only for the instructions to follow for the Interview process.

The email provides instructions to log into the Status Check portal and retrieve the date, time, and location of the interview.

The initial selection does not guarantee approval for the Green card lottery, there are further conditions to be fulfilled.

After a series of steps to complete, a consular interview determines if you will receive a visa.

Undisclosing any Family member in the form shall get disqualified. Family members refer to Spouses and Children below 21.

Kentucky Consular Center(KCC) destroys all correspondence received by mail.

A non-refundable Diversity Visa fee of $330 is mandatory during the interview process.

If selected, Winners shall receive a Sealed Immigrant Packet containing immigration documents.

The visa makes you a Lawful Permanent Resident or an American green card holder and will be permitted to work and live permanently in the United States.

There can be additional eligibility criteria for the Green Cards.




Electronic Diversity Visa Lottery Program Questions


How to fill out an EDV 2024 application form? How can I apply for the 2024 DV lottery?


- Visit dvprogram.state.gov and follow the steps to fill out the DV form 2024. Visit the EDV lottery blog for detailed procedures.

When will DV Lottery 2024 registration start? When can I apply for Green Card 2024?
- The DV Lottery 2024 shall commence at 9:45 PM on Wednesday, 5th October 2022 (19th Ashoj 2079).

Are there changes in the application form for EDV Lottery this year?
- Passports have not been made mandatory for EDV 2024 and Citizenship can be used for filling out the DV form.

Can we fill out the DV application form offline?
- As the name suggests, it is an Electronic visa type. Hence, the DV application is only available with the digital option.

When will EDV Lottery 2024 close?
- The process of EDV 2024 shall last till 8th November 2022 (22nd Kartik 2079).

How can I increase my chances of winning the DV lottery?
- There’s no such factor that really affects the probability of winning a DV lottery. Simply, filling out the DV registration form accurately can increase your chances of winning the DV lottery visas.

Which countries are eligible for DV Lottery 2024?
- Countries like Nepal, Afghanistan, Bhutan, Israel, Japan, Kuwait, Malaysia, Maldives, Qatar, Singapore, Sri Lanka, Taiwan, Thailand, etc are allowed to apply for DV this year.

DV online Application Requirements



- Applicants need to have a high school education(or equivalent), or two years of qualifying work experience to be suitable to enter the diversity visa program.

- You require a Valid Passport by your side to make sure the data on the form matches the details on the Passport. However, for 2024, Passports have not been kept mandatory.

- Make sure your country is listed in the List of qualified nations for the diversity visa.

- Other important documents required are Birth Certificates, Educational certificates, Working

- Experience certificates, Relationship documents, Medical documents, Court and Police records, etc. All these documents need to be presented during the interview in Original.

- X-rays and Vaccination records are mandatory as well.

- The Photo used for the earlier DV lottery shall not be suitable for this year’s lottery.

- All the applications before and after the mentioned duration shall be deemed ineligible.

- Applicants with multiple entries shall be disqualified, as directed by the United States government.
